TOP 6 WAYS TO PROTECT YOURSELF FROM NOVEL CORONAVIRUS COVID-19
1
Hand Wash
Wash hands throughly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds (Rub all parts of your palm).
STAY SAFE!
2
Hand Sanitise
In the absence of water/soap, use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer and properly serve your hand.
STAY HOME!
3
Healthy Lifestyle
Boost your immunity by healthy lifestyle such as eating healthy food, practicing physical activity and getting enough sleep.
STAY SAFE!
4
Social Distancing
Avoid close contact with anyone showing signs of respiratory illness and maintain social distancing.
STAY HOME!
5
Covering Mouth
When sneezing/coughing make sure to cover your mouth and nose with tissue then dispose properly. Use your elbow in the absence of tissue.
STAY SAFE!
6
Avoid Travelling
Travel only if it is essential and make sure to cover your mouth and nose with mask stay safe stay home!.
STAY HOME!
